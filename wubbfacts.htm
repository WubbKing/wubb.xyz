Fun Wubb Facts

  • A ball that looks like a kickety-kick ball appears in the 1976 Rankin-Bass holiday special Rudolph's Shiny New Year.
  • The MGM lion roar can be heard in the episode "Wubbzy in the Woods".
  • In "Widget Gets the Blooey Blues", there is a sitcom-style laughter/applause track in a few scenes. This is the only episode to do this.
  • Sounds from Galaga and Pac-Man can be heard in "Dash for Dolly".
    • More sounds from Pac-Man can also be heard in "Wubbzy and the Sparkle Stone".
  • In "Bye Bye Birdies", the song sung by the La-Dee-Da Birds is known as "Guten Abend, Gute Nacht", composed by Johannes Brahms in 1868.
    • This is a very common lullaby in the real world.
  • The music video "You Are You" is the only time we see Daizy with her hair down.
    • In the same music video, a speech balloon appears beside Walden with two graphing equations that, when graphed, reveal a smiley face.
  • Walden's computer as seen in "Wubbzy Tells a Whopper" looks very similar to the iMac G4.
