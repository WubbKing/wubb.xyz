Setting Up AIM to Work with the Phoenix Server

The old AOL IM service shut down in 2017, so it is no good anymore.

However, you can still use AIM... meet Phoenix.

Here's how to use their service:

  1. Register for an account on their website. Pick a username, forum name, and a password, use your email address, and solve the Captcha. If no error appears, you should get an authentication email in your spam box; mark it as "not spam" and verify your account.

  2. Download one of the versions of AIM here.

  3. Once your desired version of AIM is installed, patch as follows:

    1. On startup, click the Setup button (circled in the image below).

    2. Go to "Connection".

    3. Replace the URL "login.oscar.aol.com" with "iwarg.ddns.net".

  4. Enter your username and password, and click "Sign On". You should be able to add buddies and chat from then on.
